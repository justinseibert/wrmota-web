Protect the Internet

West Reading Museum of Temporary Art is a small, community-run project focused on bringing together artists, businesses, neighbors and volunteers. Our goal is to strengthen the bonds of our community and foster an appreciation for our surroundings. We could not have done it without the technology of the internet.

This project and others like it would struggle immensely or even cease to exist if forced to compete at the level of giant media conglomerates. If you are an individual or small business that relies on the internet or if you just love seeing projects like this, let your representatives know!
