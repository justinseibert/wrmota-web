Upcoming Event

We'd like to invite you to the Cherry Street Mural Stroll during West Reading's Second Friday event happening this week. When: Friday, July 13th from 5-7pm
Where: Between 4th and 7th Ave. on Cherry Street (map) The Cherry Street Mural Corridor is a collection of public art created over the past 5 years. During this time, individual artists and hundreds of community volunteers have worked together with property owners to create this unique outdoor gallery. During the Mural Stroll, you'll have a chance to meet the artists behind many of these projects. There will also be a chance to learn more about the artists and storytellers of WRMOTA and pick up a map. We hope to see you there!
